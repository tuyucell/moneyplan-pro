Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MoneyPlan Pro collects limited personal information to provide financial tracking services. This includes transaction data you manually enter and authentication details via Google Sign-In.

2. Payment Information

When you purchase a Pro subscription, payments are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We do not store or have access to your credit card or financial billing details.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used solely for organizing your personal finances. We do not sell your data to third parties. Market data is provided by external APIs to enhance your experience.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Financial records are stored securely using Supabase encryption.
